# Fees

Interstate keeps fees simple and transparent. You pay a small platform fee on trades, plus whatever the underlying network charges — and on spot trades a portion of the platform fee comes back to you as real SOL via Arena cashback.

> **TL;DR**
> - **Spot trade platform fee**: 1% of trade size, applied on entry and exit.
> - **SOL cashback**: 10% → 35% of the platform fee returned to you as real SOL, scaled by your Arena rank.
> - **Network fees**: Solana base fee + priority fee + optional MEV-protection tip — paid on-chain, not to Interstate.
> - **Deposits & withdrawals**: No Interstate fee. You only pay the Solana network cost to move SOL in or out.

---

## Platform Fees

### Spot Trading (Solana)

Interstate charges a **1% platform fee** on every spot trade — applied once when you enter a position and once when you exit. This fee funds platform development, infrastructure, and the Arena rewards program that pays a slice of it back to you.

| Trade type | Platform fee |
|---|---|
| Buy (spot) | 1% of SOL spent |
| Sell (spot) | 1% of SOL received |
| Migration sniper, limit orders, quick sell | Same 1% (no surcharge for automation) |

### Prediction Markets

Prediction market trades have their own fee structure tuned to the mechanics of yes/no markets. Fees are disclosed inline in the trade ticket before you confirm every position — there are no hidden fees. Prediction trades **do not earn SOL cashback** but do earn Credits toward your Arena rank via the prediction-market quest track.

## SOL Cashback — The Discount You Actually Earn Back

Every spot trade returns a percentage of Interstate's platform fee to you as real SOL, scaled by your Arena rank.

| Rank | Cashback % of platform fee | Effective platform fee |
|---|---|---|
| **DEGEN** | 10% | 0.90% |
| **WARRIOR** | 15% | 0.85% |
| **GLADIATOR** | 20% | 0.80% |
| **COMMANDER** | 25% | 0.75% |
| **TITAN** | 35% | 0.65% |

Cashback accumulates in your Arena balance and is claimable to your wallet as SOL at any time.

**Worked example**: You're a **COMMANDER** trading $10,000 of spot volume in a month.
- Gross platform fees: 1% × $10,000 = **$100**
- Cashback returned to you: 25% × $100 = **$25 in SOL**
- Effective platform fee: **0.75%**

For a TITAN, that same month returns $35 in SOL — an effective fee of 0.65%.

See the [Arena rewards guide](/points) for how to climb ranks.

## Network Fees (Paid to Solana, not Interstate)

When you trade on Solana, the network itself charges transaction fees. These are paid to validators, not to Interstate — we pass them through at cost.

### Base Fee

Every Solana transaction pays a **base fee of 0.000005 SOL** per signature — a fraction of a cent at current prices. This is set by the network, not Interstate.

### Priority Fee

Solana blocks have limited space. During high-traffic moments (token launches, migrations, popular trades), users compete for inclusion by paying a priority fee — a tip to validators to process your transaction first.

Interstate picks a sensible default priority fee automatically. You can override it per trade in the buy/sell ticket if you want to pay more for faster inclusion or less when the network is quiet. **Auto-fee** mode watches network congestion and adjusts dynamically.

### MEV Protection Tip (Optional)

When MEV protection is enabled, your transaction routes through a protected bundle so front-running bots and sandwich attackers can't intercept it. This costs a small **tip** on top of the base fee, typically 0.0005–0.005 SOL per trade depending on market conditions.

- **MEV protection on** — safer for large trades and high-volatility launches; small tip added.
- **MEV protection off** — cheaper, but vulnerable to sandwich attacks during volatile swaps.

You control MEV protection per trade. Interstate defaults to on for spot trades and off for low-value prediction market interactions, but you can change either default in settings.

## Deposits & Withdrawals

| Action | Interstate fee | Network cost |
|---|---|---|
| Deposit SOL into your Interstate wallet | **$0** | Solana base fee only (fractions of a cent) |
| Withdraw SOL to an external wallet | **$0** | Solana base fee only |
| Buy SOL via Coinbase integration | **$0 Interstate fee** | Coinbase's on-ramp fee applies (varies by region/payment method) |

Interstate never takes a cut of deposits or withdrawals. Your SOL is always yours, held in your own non-custodial wallet.

## Earning Yield (Marginfi Integration)

When you opt in to earn yield on idle SOL through our Marginfi integration, Marginfi's own rates and fees apply. Marginfi currently offers up to **15% APY** with instant withdrawals. Interstate charges no additional fee for routing your position — you receive the full net yield from Marginfi.

See [Marginfi's documentation](https://marginfi.com) for their most current rates and risk parameters.

## Referral Fees & Passthrough

There is no referrer fee added to a referee's trades. Instead, **Interstate gives 10% of the referee's Credits back to the referrer** — this is paid from Interstate's own reward pool, not from the referee's fees. Referring someone costs them nothing extra.
