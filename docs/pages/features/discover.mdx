# Discover — Explore Tokens

Discover is Interstate's curated browse view. While [Pulse](/features/pulse) streams every token in real time across launch/migration/post-migration states, Discover is optimized for **deliberate exploration** — finding what's moving in specific narratives, top-performing cohorts, and time-window leaderboards.

Open it at **[app.interstate.so/discover](https://app.interstate.so/discover)**.

---

## Discover vs Pulse

| | Pulse | Discover |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary use** | Reacting to live launches and migrations | Scanning for ideas across narratives and time windows |
| **Update cadence** | Sub-second live stream | Rolling time-window snapshots |
| **Best for** | Snipers, migration hunters, reactive traders | Thematic plays, late-session review, scouting narratives |
| **Data density** | High (lots of live columns) | Curated (ranked leaderboards) |

Most active traders use both: Pulse for real-time, Discover for "what actually performed today in the cat-meme category".

## What Discover Shows

### Time-Window Leaderboards
Top tokens ranked by performance within your chosen window:

- **Last 5 minutes** — very short-term momentum
- **Last hour** — session-scale movers
- **Last 24 hours** — day's biggest plays
- **Last 7 days** — broader trend view

Ranked by a composite of price change, volume growth, and holder growth — so you catch the actual winners, not just tokens pumping on thin volume.

### Narrative Clusters
Tokens grouped by narrative theme (cats, dogs, political, gaming, AI, etc.). When a narrative is trending, Discover surfaces it as a cluster so you can see the entire cohort at once rather than hunting for peers token-by-token.

Narratives are classified using metadata + our LLM tagging pipeline. New narratives get indexed within 5-10 minutes of a meaningful cohort forming.

### Cohort Rankings
Pre-built cohorts grouped by quality signal:

- **Best dev track records** — tokens launched by creators with past winners
- **Cleanest safety flags** — low sniper/insider/bundler across recently-migrated tokens
- **Highest holder growth** — tokens where organic holder count is accelerating
- **Smart-money following** — tokens where multiple tracked smart wallets have bought recently

### Recently Migrated
A dedicated view of just the last 24 hours of migrations, ordered by post-migration performance. Useful for finding tokens that successfully graduated AND had real trading activity, filtering out the 90% that migrate and die immediately.

## Filters

Same filter popout as Pulse, tuned for browse mode:

- Market cap range (min / max)
- Minimum holders
- Max dev hold, max sniper %, max bundler %
- Minimum 24h volume
- Launchpad source
- Narrative tags
- Hide tokens you already hold

Filter presets are saved per-user and travel with your account.

## Watchlist Integration

Click the star on any row to add to your [Pulse watchlist](/features/pulse#watchlist). The two features share the same watchlist — a token saved from Discover appears starred in Pulse too.

## Typical Discover Workflows

### The Theme Scout
You heard cat memes are running. Open Discover → Narrative Clusters → "Cats". The full cluster ranks by recent performance. Pick the cleanest on safety flags + healthy holder growth.

### The 24-Hour Review
End of trading session, open Discover → Last 24 hours. See what actually mattered today — biggest movers, highest volume, most migrations. Great for end-of-day journaling and spotting patterns you missed in the moment.

### The Quality Hunter
Discover → "Best dev track records" cohort. Bypass the noise of every new pump.fun launch and look only at tokens from creators who have a winning history. Much tighter funnel than Pulse's firehose.

### The Smart Money Shadow
Discover → "Smart-money following" cohort. Tokens where multiple of your [tracked wallets](/analytics/wallet-tracker) have bought recently. A single smart-money buy is noise; 3+ smart-money wallets converging is a real signal.

## Data Freshness

- Time-window leaderboards update every **30 seconds**
- Narrative clusters refresh every **5 minutes**
- Cohort rankings refresh every **2 minutes**
- Recently migrated is live (sub-second)

If you're reacting to a breaking signal, Pulse is faster. If you're investigating patterns, Discover's aggregation gives you the clearer view.
