# Portfolio

The Portfolio page is your complete view of everything you hold on Interstate: active positions, realized gains, historic trades, and claimable SOL cashback.

Open it at **[app.interstate.so/portfolio](https://app.interstate.so/portfolio)**.

---

## Overview Card

The top of the page shows your account-level numbers at a glance:

- **Total Balance** — sum of all token holdings (in USD)
- **Active SOL Balance** — spendable SOL
- **Unrealized PnL** — gain/loss on open positions if you sold right now
- **Realized PnL** — gain/loss from closed positions (lifetime)
- **24h Change** — how much your total portfolio moved today
- **SOL Cashback Balance** — accumulated Arena cashback claimable as SOL

---

## Active Positions

Every token you currently hold, with per-position detail:

| Column | What |
|---|---|
| **Token** | Avatar + name + symbol. Click → trade page. |
| **Bought** | SOL spent + USD value at entry |
| **Avg Entry** | Volume-weighted average entry price |
| **Current** | Current market cap / price |
| **Remaining** | Tokens + current USD value |
| **Unrealized PnL** | Gain/loss in USD and % |
| **Actions** | Quick sell buttons (25 / 50 / 75 / 100%) + detailed view |

Click any position row to open the **Position Detail Modal** with:
- Full trade history for this specific token
- Entry/exit markers overlaid on the price chart
- Realized vs unrealized split
- Limit orders currently active
- Dev wallet activity + safety flags

### Sorting & Filtering

- Sort by: PnL %, PnL $, size, entry date, last trade
- Filter: only winners, only losers, only above a PnL threshold
- Hide dust (default on — hides positions worth < $1)

---

## Closed Positions

Tokens you've fully exited (sold 100%). Shows:

- Total bought (SOL + USD at entry)
- Total sold (SOL + USD at exit)
- Realized PnL
- Hold duration
- Number of trades on the position

Useful for reviewing your actual win-rate and avg holding time — honest data most traders avoid.

---

## Trade History

Every individual buy and sell transaction:

- Timestamp
- Token
- Side (buy/sell)
- SOL amount
- Token amount
- Price/MCap at execution
- Platform fee paid
- Transaction signature (click to view on Solscan)

Filterable by date range, token, side, or size. Exportable to CSV for tax reporting.

---

## SOL Cashback

A dedicated panel tracks your [Arena SOL cashback](/points):

- **This season** — cashback accrued in the current Arena season
- **Lifetime** — all-time cashback earned
- **Claimable** — unclaimed cashback available to withdraw to your wallet
- **Claim** button — moves SOL from your Arena balance to your Interstate wallet

Cashback is claimable at any time. No minimum, no cooldown, no approval step.

---

## Referral Earnings

If you have referees, this panel shows:

- Total referral passthrough Credits earned (lifetime)
- This season's passthrough
- Recruiter milestone bonuses unlocked
- Active referee count

See the full [Referral Program](/getting-started/referral-program) for mechanics.

---

## Analytics Tab

Deep performance metrics on your trading (only shows if you have enough history):

- **Win rate** — % of closed positions that ended in profit
- **Avg hold time** — mean time from entry to exit
- **Best / worst trade** — your PR and your disaster
- **Breakdown by protocol** — are you doing better on pump.fun vs post-migration DEX?
- **Breakdown by dev track record** — win rate on first-time devs vs repeat launchers
- **Streak** — longest profitable streak, current streak
- **Time-of-day heatmap** — when you trade vs when you make money

Most of these metrics become meaningful after 50+ closed positions. Use them to find your own edge (or confirm you don't have one and should size down).

---

## Tax Export

Click **Export Transactions** to download your complete trade history as CSV with columns ready for common tax tools (Koinly, CoinTracker, etc.).

The export includes:
- Timestamp (UTC)
- Asset bought/sold
- USD values at execution
- Fees paid
- Transaction signature

**Important**: Interstate does not provide tax advice. Use this export with a qualified tax professional in your jurisdiction.

---

## Multi-Chain Portfolios

If you've traded on multiple chains, each chain has its own Portfolio tab:

- **Solana** (primary)
- **Monad** (if you've used Monad trading)
- Additional chains as they're added

Each tab shows isolated data for that chain. There's no unified cross-chain portfolio view today — manage each side separately.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| `P` | Jump to Portfolio from anywhere |
| `1–5` | Switch tabs (Positions / Closed / History / Cashback / Analytics) |
| `/` | Focus search/filter input |
| `E` | Export current view to CSV |
