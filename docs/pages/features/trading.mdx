## Chart

Interstate uses **TradingView-compatible OHLCV charts** powered by our own indexer — trade ticks are live via WebSocket, no 30-second polling lag.

**Supported timeframes**: 1s, 5s, 15s, 1m, 5m, 15m, 30m, 1h, 4h, 1d, 1w

**Chart tools:**
- Drawing tools (trendlines, rectangles, fib retracements)
- Volume histogram (default on)
- Dev wallet markers (see where the creator bought/sold)
- MCap toggle (switch between price and market cap Y-axis)

The chart data comes from your own indexer — the same one that powers Pulse — so candles reflect the state of the bonding curve or DEX pool in real time. No stale prices.

## Buy / Sell Panel

### Buying

1. Toggle **Buy**
2. Enter amount in SOL (or use percent presets: 10%, 25%, 50%, 100% of wallet)
3. Review:
   - Estimated tokens received (post-slippage)
   - Platform fee (1% — see [Fees](/fees))
   - Network fee + MEV tip
4. Hit **Buy**

The transaction signs invisibly via your Turnkey MPC wallet. Lands on-chain in 1–2 seconds under normal conditions.

### Selling

1. Toggle **Sell**
2. Choose percentage (25%, 50%, 75%, 100%) or enter exact token amount
3. Same fee review → **Sell**

The **Quick Sell** preset buttons in the Positions panel let you exit in one tap without opening the full trade ticket — useful when a position is crashing.

## Positions Panel

Every token you currently hold shows here, with:

- **Bought** (SOL + USD value at entry)
- **Sold** (if partial exits)
- **Remaining** (tokens + current USD value)
- **PnL** (realized + unrealized)
- **Actions** — quick sell, view details, set limit order

Click any row to open **Position Detail Modal** with full trade history, avg entry, realized/unrealized split, and a mini-chart of your entry/exit points overlaid on price.

---

## Holders Table

Shows the top 50 holders of the token, with per-wallet flags:

- **Dev** — the creator wallet
- **Sniper** — bought within 3 slots of creation (bot-like behavior)
- **Insider** — received tokens via transfer (not bought on market)
- **Bundler** — part of a coordinated bundle buy (4+ wallets same slot)
- **Copy trader** — suspected follower wallet

Each flag is computed by our [detection engine](/analytics/bundle-detection) in real time. Hover any flag for details on why it was assigned.

---

## Top Traders

Ranks the top 10 sellers by realized PnL on this specific token. Great for spotting whales who've successfully extracted value — you can click through to track their wallet.

The table shows: wallet address (abbreviated), total bought / sold, realized PnL in USD, win-rate on similar tokens.

---

## Recent Trades

Live feed of the last 1,000 trades on the token. Each row: timestamp, buy/sell, SOL size, price, wallet address.

Colored rows let you scan quickly:
- **Green** = buys
- **Red** = sells
- **Yellow** = flagged wallet (sniper / insider / bundler)

Useful for reading flow: if you see 10 consecutive red rows of 0.5+ SOL, something's dumping.

---

## Dev Tokens

Shows other tokens the creator has deployed — their success rate, how long those tokens lasted, whether they rugged. A creator with 5 past rugs is a different risk profile than a first-time deployer.

This panel answers: **"Has this dev made money for anyone before?"**

---

## Limit Orders

Bottom-right panel lists your active limit orders on this token:

- **Stop loss** (auto-sell if price drops below X)
- **Take profit** (auto-sell if price hits Y)
- **Limit buy** (auto-buy when price drops to Z)

See [Limit Orders](/automation/limit-orders) for how to set them up and what triggers they support.

## Settings That Matter

In the trade ticket's settings menu (gear icon):

- **Default slippage** — Applies to every trade unless overridden
- **Default priority fee** — Auto / manual
- **MEV protection default** — on / off
- **Confirmation prompt** — require double-confirm for trades > X SOL
- **Quick sell presets** — customize the 25/50/75/100 buttons to your preferred split

---

## Multi-Chain

Interstate supports **Solana** as primary chain. Additional chains roll out periodically:

- **Monad** — integrated, see `/bnb` page
- **BNB** — integrated with limited pools
- Additional chains coming

The chain switcher at the top of the UI toggles contexts. Each chain has its own wallet balance and trade ticket — amounts are always in the native gas token (SOL on Solana, etc.).

---

## Performance Notes

- Trade confirmations typically arrive in **1–2 seconds** when Solana isn't congested
- During peak congestion (major launches), priority fee matters — see [Quick Start Tips](/getting-started/quickstart-tips)
- WebSocket price feed latency is sub-second from on-chain event to your chart
- If the chart freezes, refresh the page — the WS reconnects automatically
