# Telegram Bot

The Interstate Telegram bot is your trading cockpit on the go. Connect it once, and your tracked wallets, tweet monitors, limit orders, and Arena quest progress all flow directly into your Telegram chat — no need to keep the web app open.

---

## What the Bot Does

### 1. Real-Time Trade Alerts

When a trade triggers on your account, the bot sends you an instant notification:

- **Limit order filled** — "Your take-profit on $WIF just executed at $3.45M MCap for +62%"
- **Stop-loss triggered** — "Stop-loss on $TRUMP fired at -40% MCap"
- **Migration sniper fired** — "Sniper bought 0.1 SOL of $BONK at migration, now tracking"
- **Dev-sell trigger** — "⚠️ Dev wallet sold 5% supply on $PEPE. Auto-sell fired."

You can fine-tune which events trigger alerts — see **Notification Settings** below.

### 2. Wallet Tracker Pings

If you have wallets on your [Wallet Tracker](/analytics/wallet-tracker), every trade they make can route straight to your Telegram:

- "Smart Money #1 bought 2.5 SOL of $TOKEN at $120K MCap"
- "Alpha Wallet sold 100% of $MEME — check their activity"

One-tap from the notification → opens the trade page directly on **app.interstate.so** (or via in-bot quick-buy if you have that enabled).

### 3. Twitter Monitor Alerts

The bot forwards tweets from your tracked X/Twitter accounts, including any Solana CAs it extracts automatically:

- "🐦 @AlphaCaller just tweeted about $PEPE: [tweet preview] — Trade now"
- One-tap Quick Buy button launches you into the app at the mentioned token

See [Twitter Monitor](/analytics/twitter-monitor) for which accounts to track.

### 4. Arena Quest Tracking

Daily and seasonal [Arena quest](/points) progress lives in the bot, too:

- New daily quests refresh at midnight UTC — bot sends you a quick snapshot
- Big seasonal milestone reached? Bot delivers a congratulations + Credits earned
- Streak in danger of breaking? Bot pings you an hour before midnight to protect it

### 5. Social Rewards

Key tweet RT's, community channel joins, and other one-time Credit drops — the bot confirms each one as it happens so you know your Arena balance is updating.

### 6. Daily & Weekly Summaries

Opt into daily or weekly digest messages:

- **Daily summary** — trades executed, PnL realized, rank progress, Credits earned
- **Weekly summary** — leaderboard position, cashback balance, top wallets you tracked, highlights

Easy way to see your activity at a glance without logging into the app.

## Connecting the Bot

1. Open Interstate → **Settings** → **Notifications** → **Telegram**
2. Click **Connect Telegram** → opens the bot (@InterstateTradingBot) in a new tab
3. Start a chat with the bot → it'll show a one-time link code
4. Enter the code into the Interstate web app (or just tap **"/link"** in the bot)
5. Confirmation appears in both places — you're connected

Linking is **two-way** — your Telegram account is tied to your Interstate account, and vice versa. Disconnect at any time from the same settings page.

## Notification Settings

Fine-grain control over what the bot pings you about. In **Settings → Notifications**:

| Event | Default | Recommended |
|---|---|---|
| Limit order filled | ✅ on | Leave on |
| Migration sniper triggered | ✅ on | Leave on |
| Dev-sell trigger fired | ✅ on | Leave on — this is your panic-exit signal |
| Wallet tracker trade | ✅ on (for starred wallets) | Fine-tune per wallet |
| Twitter monitor tweet | ⚠️ off by default | Enable for top 5 accounts only |
| Daily quest refresh | ⚠️ off by default | On if you care about dailies |
| Streak warning | ✅ on | Leave on |
| Daily digest | ⚠️ off | Try it for a week |
| Weekly digest | ✅ on | Good habit-builder |

You can also set **quiet hours** (e.g., 11pm–7am in your timezone) during which no notifications fire.

## Security & Privacy

### What the Bot Has Access To

- **Notification data** — trade events, quest progress, rank updates
- **Public wallet data** — address, balance, positions (same as what's on your app)
- **Arena progress** — Credits, streak, rank, referrals

### What the Bot Does NOT Have Access To

- **Your private key** — trading actions triggered from the bot still require signing through the web app or your wallet (the bot doesn't hold or transmit keys)
- **Your authentication password** — link is one-way; the bot can push info, not pull credentials
- **Full trade history of other users** — only your own account's data

Unlink the bot at any time from the Interstate settings page. Doing so stops all notifications immediately and revokes the bot's ability to read your account.

### Watch Out for Impersonators

The **only official bot handle is `@InterstateTradingBot`**. Any other Telegram account claiming to be Interstate is a scam. Do NOT:

- Share your private key or seed phrase with any Telegram account
- Click links from DMs claiming to be Interstate support
- Run commands from bots other than the official one

Real Interstate staff never DM you first. Official support is in the [community Telegram channel](https://t.me/+DDXGrsJoe3szYTAx), not via DM.

## Troubleshooting

**"The bot isn't responding"** — Make sure you've linked your account. Try `/start` to re-initialize. If still silent, contact [Support](/support).

**"I'm getting duplicate notifications"** — Sometimes a multi-wallet setup can double-fire if two wallets are linked to the same Telegram. Unlink one wallet's notifications.

**"I want notifications only for large trades"** — Set a minimum USD threshold in Settings → Notifications → Tracker alerts.

**"Bot commands say 'not linked'"** — Your Telegram account isn't linked to an Interstate account. Go through the connection flow in the web app first.
