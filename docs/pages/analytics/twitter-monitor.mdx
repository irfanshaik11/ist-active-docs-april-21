# Twitter Monitor

Twitter Monitor tracks the X/Twitter accounts you care about and pings you the moment any of them post, reply, or retweet — with automatic token-mention extraction so you can act on alpha in seconds, not minutes.

---

## How It Works

Most meme-coin pumps trace back to a specific tweet — a known alpha caller posting a CA, a key influencer mentioning a token, or a founder announcing a narrative. By the time you refresh X manually and scroll past unrelated posts, the first 2× has already happened.

Interstate monitors your selected X accounts continuously. When they post:
1. The tweet text is scanned for Solana mint addresses, ticker symbols, and known narratives.
2. If a token is mentioned, the tweet + the token's current price / Pulse row are pushed to your feed instantly.
3. You can buy with one click directly from the notification.

Typical latency from tweet posted → Interstate notification is **under 5 seconds**.

---

## Setting Up

1. Open **Trackers** → **Twitter** tab.
2. Click **Add Account**.
3. Paste the X/Twitter handle (with or without the `@`).
4. Label the account (e.g., "Alpha Caller #1", "Protocol Founder").
5. Choose notification preferences (in-app, Telegram, sound, push).

The tracker is live immediately. Any new post, reply, or retweet from that account is captured.

---

## Automatic Token Extraction

When a tracked account posts, Interstate runs NLP + pattern-matching against the text to find:

- **Solana mint addresses** — 32-44 char Base58 strings
- **$TICKER mentions** — `$PEPE`, `$WIF`, etc.
- **Known token names** — matched against our indexed token database
- **Contract addresses in image OCR** — some alpha callers post CAs as screenshots

Extracted tokens appear as **clickable badges** in the notification. Tap → jump straight to the trade page.

---

## Filters

Track a lot of accounts and want to cut noise? Use filters:

- **Only posts containing a token mention** (skip personal tweets)
- **Only original posts** (skip retweets)
- **Only first-time mentions** (skip when they keep re-tweeting the same token)
- **Minimum follower count of mentioned token** (if we have it indexed)
- **Exclude replies** (some accounts are noise in replies)

Filters are per-account. Your top 3 critical accounts might get "notify on everything"; your 20 watched accounts might get "notify only on new token mentions".

Combine Twitter Monitor with [Limit Orders](/automation/limit-orders) for hands-free alpha response:

1. A specific account tweets a Solana CA

- Stop-loss paired in advance

---

## Limitations

- **X API rate limits** affect how fast we can detect tweets. Typical latency 2–5 seconds.
- **Tweet deletion** — if an account deletes a tweet, we retain it in your feed (the data was already captured) with a "deleted" badge.
- **Private / protected accounts** — not trackable (X API restriction).
- **Image OCR** — best effort, not guaranteed to catch all image-based CAs.
