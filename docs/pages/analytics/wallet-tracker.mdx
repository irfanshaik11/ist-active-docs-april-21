# Wallet Tracker

Wallet Tracker lets you follow specific Solana wallets and receive live notifications every time they trade. Combine it with [Limit Orders](/automation/limit-orders) and you have a copy-trade setup without writing a single line of code.

Open it at **[app.interstate.so/trackers](https://app.interstate.so/trackers)**.

---

## Why Track Wallets?

Certain wallets consistently outperform. Dev wallets of past-successful tokens, well-known alpha callers, consistent profitable snipers — their trade flow often leads retail by minutes. Following them:

- Surfaces new tokens earlier than Pulse can (because they hit these wallets before the filters catch them)
- Lets you validate your own thesis ("is a smart wallet already in this?")
- Provides a free signal source without writing scraping bots

---

## Adding a Wallet

1. Open **Trackers** from the top-nav or sidebar.
2. Click **Add Wallet**.
3. Paste the Solana address.
4. Add a **label** (e.g., "Smart Money #1", "Dev of last rug").
5. Choose notification preferences:
   - **All trades**
   - **Only buys >$X** (filter noise)
   - **Only on tokens you don't hold** (avoid re-signaling known positions)

The wallet now appears in your Trackers list. Every trade it makes shows up in your **Activity Feed** within seconds of landing on-chain.

---

## Activity Feed

The tracker activity feed shows:

| Column | What |
|---|---|
| Wallet label | Your custom label for the wallet |
| Action | Buy / Sell |
| Token | Mint + current Pulse row |
| Size | SOL / USD value |
| Time | Seconds ago, live updating |
| Quick buy | One-tap button to mirror the trade |

You can filter the feed by: wallet, token, buy-only, sell-only, or size threshold.

## Notifications

When a tracked wallet trades, you can choose how to be notified:

- **In-app toast** (default) — popup in the corner of the UI
- **Sound alert** — audible ping
- **Telegram** — connect the Interstate Telegram bot and receive direct messages
- **Push notification** (on mobile app)

Each wallet has its own notification settings, so you can get Telegram pings for your top 3 wallets and silent feed-only for the rest.

---

## Copy Trade Mode

For your highest-confidence tracked wallets, enable **Copy Trade Mode**:

- Every buy they make triggers an auto-buy from your wallet
- You set a **proportion** (e.g., 10% of their trade size, capped at 0.1 SOL per token)
- Optional filters: only copy if passes bundle-detection thresholds, only under X MCap, etc.
- Paired auto-sells trigger on their exits

**Caveats**:
- You never mirror faster than 1 block after them (the chain enforces ordering)
- If they're sniping migrations, their fill often lands in a slot you can't compete with
- Pure copy-trading = pure dependence; tracked wallets can turn bad

## Finding Wallets Worth Tracking

### From the Token Trade Page
Every token's **Top Traders** panel shows the top 10 sellers by realized PnL on that token. Click any wallet → "Add to tracker". Now you're tracking proven winners on the tokens you care about.

### From Pulse
Hover any trade in the "Recent trades" feed. Wallets that appear repeatedly across multiple successful tokens are candidates.

### From External Lists
Public leaderboards like Birdeye's top wallets, GMGN's trader rankings, or niche Telegram channels often publish lists of profitable wallets. Paste them into Trackers.

### Your Own Cohort
Track a friend's wallet to see their entries / exits in real time. Useful for trading together without voice chat.

## Copy-Trader Detection

When you add a wallet, Interstate automatically checks whether it's been flagged as a **copy-trader** itself — meaning the wallet primarily follows other wallets rather than finding opportunities independently.

Copy-trader wallets aren't worth tracking because:
- They add latency (you're copying a copier, who's copying someone smarter)
- The actual alpha is upstream

If Interstate flags a wallet you're tracking as a copy-trader, it'll suggest the upstream wallet to track instead.

## Use Cases

### The Alpha Scout
Track 5–10 known smart wallets. Use notifications only (no copy-trade). When multiple smart wallets buy the same token within a short window, you get a "cluster alert" — a strong signal to dig in.

### The Risk-Off Exit Signal
Track the dev wallets of tokens you hold. The moment a dev wallet sells, your Telegram lights up. Pair with a [dev-sell limit order](/automation/limit-orders) and you're both notified and out of the position.

### The Full Copy-Trader
Identify one truly consistent wallet (not easy). Enable copy-trade with 10–20% proportion. You mirror their winners and losers in equal proportion — the expected value is positive only if your target wallet is actually skilled.

### The Portfolio Mirror
Track your friend group's wallets. See who's in what. Nobody has to broadcast their trades in chat — it's all visible in real time.
