# Quick Start

Interstate is a hybrid Solana trading platform and wallet. This guide gets you from zero to your first trade in about three minutes.

> **TL;DR**
> 1. Go to [app.interstate.so](https://app.interstate.so) and sign in.
> 2. A non-custodial wallet is created for you automatically — no seed phrase to back up manually.
> 3. Deposit SOL (or buy via Coinbase on-ramp).
> 4. Open the [Pulse](/features/pulse) page, pick a token, and trade in one click.

---

## Step 1 — Sign In

Go to **[app.interstate.so](https://app.interstate.so)** and choose how you want to sign in:

| Method | What you get |
|---|---|
| **Google** | Fastest — uses your Google account, Interstate creates a Turnkey MPC wallet behind the scenes. |
| **Email + Password** | Classic flow. Same Turnkey MPC wallet is created. |
| **Phantom / Solflare** | Connect your existing Solana wallet directly. |
| **MetaMask** | EVM signing for cross-chain support (EVM chains coming). |

New users get assigned a **waitlist spot** — see [waitlist/status](/faqs) for how invites flow. Once activated, your full dashboard is live.

---

## Step 2 — Your Wallet

Interstate wallets are **non-custodial** — your private key is held in a [Turnkey](https://turnkey.com) MPC sub-organization tied to your account. Interstate never has custody of your funds.

You can see your public address at the top of the screen. Click it to:

- **Deposit** — shows a QR code and address for receiving SOL
- **Withdraw** — send SOL to any external wallet (no Interstate fee, only Solana network cost)
- **Export** — export your private key if you ever want to migrate (power-user feature, guard carefully)
- **Buy crypto** — on-ramp via Coinbase (up to $500/week without KYC depending on region)

---

## Step 3 — Fund Your Wallet

You have three options to get SOL into your Interstate wallet:

1. **Send SOL from another wallet** — Copy your Interstate public address, send SOL from Phantom / Solflare / an exchange / a friend. Shows up in ~10 seconds.
2. **Coinbase on-ramp** — Click "Buy crypto" → purchase up to $500/week in SOL with card/bank. No KYC under that threshold.
3. **Import an existing wallet** — If you already have a Solana wallet with SOL, import the private key or seed phrase to use it directly on Interstate.

A SOL balance of 0.05–0.1 SOL is enough to start small trades and cover network fees.

---

## Step 4 — Find a Token

The **[Pulse](/features/pulse)** page (`/pulse?chain=sol`) shows live token flow across three columns:

- **New** — tokens that just launched on pump.fun / Meteora / Bonk / Moonit / Raydium Launchpad
- **Final Stretch** — tokens near migration (high bonding curve %, building volume)
- **Migrated** — tokens that successfully moved to a DEX (Raydium, DAMM V2, PumpSwap)

Each row shows: market cap, holder count, dev's holding, sniper %, insider %, bundler %, and a live mini-chart. Hover for tooltips, click to open the full trade page.

You can also search any mint address directly in the search bar.

---

## Step 5 — Your First Trade

On any token's trade page:

1. **Pick side** — Buy or Sell toggle.
2. **Enter amount** — In SOL for buys, percentage of your position for sells.
3. **Check fees** — Platform fee, priority fee, optional MEV protection tip all shown inline.
4. **Hit Buy / Sell** — Transaction signs via Turnkey (invisible to you), lands on-chain in ~1–2 seconds.

Your position instantly appears in the **Positions** panel below the chart. Every trade earns **Credits** toward your Arena rank — see [how Credits work](/points).

## Common First-Day Issues

**"Transaction failed"** — Usually a priority fee too low during congestion. Turn on Auto-Fee in trade settings, or manually bump priority fee to 0.001 SOL.

**"Slippage exceeded"** — Token price moved more than your slippage tolerance between quote and execution. Increase slippage to 10–20% for volatile launches.

**"Insufficient SOL"** — You need a small buffer (~0.01 SOL) above your trade amount to cover network fees.

**"Wallet not showing balance"** — Give it ~15 seconds after a deposit for the blockchain to finalize and Interstate to re-scan.
