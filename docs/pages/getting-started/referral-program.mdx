# Referral Program

Refer friends to Interstate and you earn a permanent 10% of every Credit they ever earn — for the lifetime of their account. Layered on top: one-time recruiter milestone bonuses each time your referral network crosses an activity threshold.

The referral system is the single highest-leverage way to stack passive Credits without trading yourself.

---

## How It Works

### 10% Passthrough — Forever

When someone you referred earns Credits from trading, **10% of their base Credits flows back to you automatically.** No caps. No time limit. No action needed.

- The passthrough is always on the referee's **base Credits** (pre-multiplier)
- Your own rank multiplier then applies on top of the passthrough amount
- There is no cost to them — Interstate pays this from its own reward pool

**Worked example:** Your friend becomes an active trader and earns 50,000 base Credits over their first season through trading.

- 50,000 × 10% = **5,000 base Credits** from passthrough
- Your multiplier applies on top of that. As a **WARRIOR II** (2×): 5,000 × 2 = **10,000 Credits**.
- As a **TITAN**: 5,000 × 4 = **20,000 Credits**.

And this isn't one-time — it keeps flowing every time they trade.

### Recruiter Milestones

On top of the ongoing 10% passthrough, you unlock **one-time bonus Credits** each time your referral network crosses a new activity milestone. Multiple escalating tiers:

- Small bonuses for your first recruits
- Larger ones as the network grows
- Top-tier milestones pay enough Credits to jump multiple rank levels in a single season

Someone who genuinely commits to community building — bringing in a real crew of active Interstate traders — can pick up enough from recruiter milestones alone to outpace high-volume solo traders.

## How to Refer Someone

1. Go to your **Arena** page → **Referrals** tab.
2. Copy your unique referral code or direct link.
3. Share it however you want — group chats, Twitter, Discord, in-person.
4. When someone signs up with your code, they're permanently linked to you.

That's it. No approval step, no verification delay. The moment they execute their first trade, your passthrough starts flowing.

## Tracking Your Referrals

The Referrals tab in Arena shows:

- **Active referees** — number of users linked to you who've traded at least once
- **Total passthrough Credits earned** — all-time lifetime
- **Passthrough this season**
- **Next milestone** — Credits your network needs to accumulate for the next bonus
- **Per-referee view** — each referee's Credits earned, cashback stacked, trading activity

You can see which referees are active, which have gone quiet, and nudge accordingly.

## Why This System Is Different

Most referral programs in crypto do one of two things:

1. **One-time signup bonus** — referrer gets X Credits when referee creates an account. No ongoing benefit.
2. **Fee kickback** — referrer gets a cut of the referee's platform fees. Creates an adversarial relationship (referrer wins when referee pays more).

Interstate's system avoids both:
- **Not one-time** — the passthrough is ongoing for the referee's entire lifetime
- **Not fee-based** — the referee pays nothing extra; their fees are identical whether they were referred or not
- **Additive, not extractive** — the Credits you earn come from Interstate's reward pool, not from your referee's pocket

You're incentivized to bring in users who will actually trade and stick around — not churn through signups.

## Fair Use Rules

To keep the program fair and prevent abuse:

- **One account per person.** Multi-accounting to self-refer is prohibited and will result in forfeiture of all rewards (from both the main and sock-puppet accounts).
- **Genuine referrals only.** Sybil attacks, paid-signup farms, or bot-farm referral schemes will be detected and disqualified.
- **Existing users can't be retroactively attached** to a referrer. Referral codes only work on new signups.

Violation of fair use rules may result in:
- Loss of accumulated referral bonuses
- Permanent ineligibility for recruiter milestones
- Account suspension

## Referral FAQ

**Q: Do I need to be high-rank to refer?**
A: No. Referring works from the moment you have an Interstate account, even at DEGEN.

**Q: What happens if my referee stops trading?**
A: You stop earning passthrough from them. Anything you earned before is yours forever.

**Q: Can I have unlimited referees?**
A: Yes. No cap on network size.

**Q: Can someone I referred refer others?**
A: Yes — Interstate has single-level referrals. The people your referee refers earn Credits for *themselves*, but their passthrough goes to their direct referrer (your referee), not to you.

**Q: Does the referee know they were referred by me?**
A: Yes — their signup flow shows which referrer they're linked to. The referee can also see this in their Arena page.

**Q: Can I change my referral code?**
A: Referral codes are tied to your account and are not changeable. But you can copy the share link anywhere.
