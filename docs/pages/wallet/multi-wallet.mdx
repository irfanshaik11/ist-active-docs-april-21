# Multi-Wallet

Interstate supports running multiple wallets under a single account. Each wallet has its own isolated balance, positions, trade history, and limit orders — useful for separating strategies, keeping risk capital separate from long-term holdings, or running paper-trade experiments alongside live capital.

---

## Your Wallet Setup

When you sign up, Interstate creates **one primary wallet** for you — a non-custodial Turnkey MPC wallet tied to your account. From there you can add:

- **Additional Interstate-generated wallets** — new Turnkey MPC wallets under your account, each with a unique address
- **Imported external wallets** — bring in a Solana wallet you already own (Phantom, Solflare, hardware wallet) via private key or seed phrase
- **EVM wallets** — separate MetaMask / imported keys for EVM-chain trading

All wallets live under your account's umbrella but are cryptographically independent. Transactions, balances, and positions don't commingle.

---

## Creating a New Wallet

1. Click your wallet address at the top of any page → **Manage Wallets**
2. Click **Add Wallet** → **Generate New**
3. Name the wallet (e.g., "Snipe Fund", "Long-term Hold", "Paper Trade")
4. Interstate creates a new Turnkey MPC sub-wallet instantly

The new wallet appears in your wallet switcher with zero balance. Fund it by:
- Sending SOL from your primary wallet
- Using [Buy Crypto](/features/buy-crypto) with the new wallet selected
- Receiving SOL from an external source

## Importing an External Wallet

If you already have a Solana wallet with funds or history you want to use on Interstate:

1. **Manage Wallets** → **Add Wallet** → **Import Existing**
2. Choose input method:
   - **Private key** (Base58 format — 88 characters)
   - **Seed phrase** (12 or 24 words, BIP39)
   - **Keystore JSON** (encrypted backup file)
3. Name the imported wallet
4. Interstate stores the key encrypted — only your account password / Turnkey session decrypts it

The imported wallet's full on-chain history (balances, past trades, positions) becomes visible in Interstate's UI immediately. Any Solana tokens you already hold show up in your Portfolio.

### Import Considerations

- **Your imported key lives alongside our Turnkey wallet infrastructure.** The private key is encrypted with your account credentials.
- **Any trades on the imported wallet sign from that key** — Turnkey is not involved for imported wallets.
- **Exporting** — you can export the private key at any time.
- **Revocation** — removing the imported wallet from Interstate doesn't affect the on-chain wallet itself; it just hides it from your UI.

---

## Exporting a Wallet

1. **Manage Wallets** → select the wallet → **Export**
2. Verify your account password
3. Export as:
   - Base58 private key (compatible with Phantom, Solflare, any Solana wallet)
   - Seed phrase (if one was generated)
   - Keystore JSON

Exporting is **irreversible from a trust standpoint** — once the key leaves Interstate's encrypted storage, anyone with a copy can drain the wallet. Store exports offline.

## Wallet Switcher

The wallet switcher at the top of Interstate lets you flip between wallets instantly:

- Click your avatar → wallet list appears
- Tap any wallet → the entire UI context switches (Portfolio, Pulse filters, open orders, positions)
- Each wallet has its own balance, open positions, and trade history
- Your **Arena account** is shared across all wallets (Credits, rank, cashback all accumulate in your single Arena profile)

**Key distinction**: your Arena progression is per-account, not per-wallet. Trading from any of your wallets counts toward the same Credits pool and rank.

## Common Multi-Wallet Strategies

### Separate Risk Capital from Savings
- **Wallet 1 ("Trading")** — small balance, used for aggressive meme trades
- **Wallet 2 ("Savings")** — larger balance, used only for long-term holds / yield via [Marginfi](/fees)

Switching wallets before each trade enforces the separation. You can't accidentally dip into savings for a speculative trade.

### Strategy Isolation
- **Wallet 1 ("Sniper")** — runs migration snipers, automated rules fire here
- **Wallet 2 ("Manual")** — all manual trades, cleaner trade history for review

Makes post-trade analysis easier — winning / losing strategies are not mixed.

### Paper Trading Alongside Live
- **Wallet 1 ("Live")** — real capital
- **Wallet 2 ("Paper")** — funded with tiny amounts, used to test ideas without meaningful risk

You can run both simultaneously and compare performance.

### Tax Bucketing
- **Wallet 1** — long-term positions (held > 1 year for favorable tax treatment in some jurisdictions)
- **Wallet 2** — short-term speculative

Simpler tax reporting at year-end.

## Limits

- **DEGEN tier**: 1 primary wallet (default)
- **WARRIOR+**: up to 3 wallets (primary + 2 additional)
- **GLADIATOR+**: up to 5 wallets
- **COMMANDER+**: up to 10 wallets
- **TITAN**: unlimited

Wallet limits scale with [Arena rank](/points). Imported wallets count against your limit.

---

## Security Notes

- **Each wallet is cryptographically independent.** Compromising one doesn't expose the others.
- **Turnkey MPC wallets** are generated fresh each time (no shared seed across your multi-wallet set) — this is an intentional security property.
- **Imported wallets** carry whatever security story they had before import. If you imported a hot wallet that's been exposed elsewhere, the import inherits that exposure.
- **Your account credentials** (email / OAuth / password) govern access to the wallet switcher. Lose your account → lose access to all wallets. Export + offline backup if you hold meaningful value.

See [Benefits & Risks](/benefits-and-risks) for the full wallet security discussion.
