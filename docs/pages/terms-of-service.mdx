## Trading risks

Crypto trading carries real risk, including but not limited to:

- Rapid loss of value
- Smart contract bugs or exploits in the tokens you trade

- Failed transactions that still consume fees
- Chain congestion causing slippage above your configured threshold
- Third-party service outages

Interstate surfaces risk signals — bundle detection, sniper %, holder concentration, duplicate-image warnings — but these are aids, not guarantees.
