# Privacy Policy

:::warning
**Draft — requires legal review before publishing.** This is a starting-point template based on Interstate's actual data practices. Please have counsel review and tailor to your jurisdiction before publishing to production.
:::

_Last updated: 2026-04-21_

This Privacy Policy describes how Interstate ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services at **app.interstate.so**, related websites, APIs, and browser/mobile clients (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account information**: email address, display name, password (hashed), authentication method (Google OAuth, email/password, Phantom, MetaMask).
- **Wallet details**: public wallet addresses you connect or that are generated for you. We do not directly hold your private keys — they are held by our wallet partner, [Turnkey](https://turnkey.com), in an MPC sub-organization tied to your account.
- **Profile & social connections**: X (Twitter), Telegram, Discord handles you choose to connect for Arena rewards.
- **Communications**: any messages you send to support, community channels, or our team.
- **Referral data**: referral codes you enter or generate.

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically

- **On-chain activity**: all public blockchain transactions you execute through the Services (Solana, and future supported chains). This data is, by nature, already public on the blockchain.
- **Device & usage data**: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, referring URLs, pages viewed, time spent, and interaction events.
- **Performance data**: API response times, error logs, and feature usage patterns (aggregated and anonymized where possible).
- **Cookies & local storage**: session tokens, user preferences, and authentication state.

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Wallet providers** (Turnkey, Phantom, MetaMask): wallet addresses and signed-message proof of ownership.
- **On-ramp providers** (Coinbase, others): transaction confirmations (not underlying KYC / personal data — that stays with them).
- **Yield providers** (Marginfi, others): position and yield information.
- **Social platforms** (X/Twitter, when you link your account): public profile information and engagement events relevant to Arena rewards.
- **Analytics & fraud detection** (third-party services): aggregated behavioral and threat data.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Services.
- Execute trades you initiate and manage your positions, orders, and balances.
- Administer the Arena rewards program (credits, ranks, cashback, referrals).
- Detect and prevent fraud, manipulation, and abuse (including bundle/sniper/insider detection, bot activity, and suspicious account patterns).
- Communicate with you — including service announcements, feature updates, and (where you've consented) marketing.
- Improve the Services through analytics and usage measurement.
- Comply with legal obligations and enforce our [Terms of Service](/terms-of-service).

---

## 3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as described below:

- **With service providers** that help us operate the Services (cloud hosting, wallet custody, analytics, customer support). These providers access only the data needed to perform their functions and are contractually required to protect it.
- **With blockchain networks**: any transaction you make is broadcast to a public blockchain and visible to anyone.
- **With third-party integrations** you explicitly connect (e.g., Coinbase on-ramp, Marginfi yield).
- **For legal compliance**: when required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
- **To protect rights and safety**: to prevent fraud, enforce terms, or protect users, Interstate, or the public.
- **In a business transaction**: during a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to continued privacy protection.

## 4. Your Choices

### 4.1 Account & Profile
You can update your email, display name, and connected social handles from your Account page. You can disconnect social integrations at any time.

### 4.2 Wallet
You can export your wallet private key from your Account page (only if you opt in). Once exported, you are solely responsible for its safekeeping.

### 4.3 Communications
You can opt out of marketing emails via the unsubscribe link in any marketing message. Service-critical emails (security alerts, transaction confirmations, account changes) cannot be disabled as long as you use the Services.

### 4.4 Data Deletion
You can request deletion of your account by contacting support. Note:
- **On-chain transactions are immutable** and cannot be deleted — they are public by design.
- Some data may be retained for legal, accounting, or fraud-prevention purposes as permitted by law.
- Account deletion typically takes up to 30 days.

### 4.5 Jurisdiction-Specific Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under laws like GDPR (EU/UK), CCPA (California), or similar regimes:
- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data (subject to legal exceptions)
- **Restrict** or **object** to certain processing
- **Portability** of data
- **Withdraw consent** where processing is consent-based

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [privacy@interstate.so](mailto:privacy@interstate.so).

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the Services and comply with legal obligations. Generally:

- **Account data**: kept while the account is active, then deleted per our retention schedule after closure.
- **Transaction logs**: kept for at least 7 years for legal/accounting reasons.
- **Anonymized analytics**: may be kept indefinitely.

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards — including encryption in transit (TLS), access controls, MPC wallet custody via Turnkey, and monitoring for unusual activity. **No system is perfectly secure.** You share responsibility by using a strong password, enabling 2FA on your authentication provider, and safeguarding any exported private keys.

## 7. Children's Privacy
