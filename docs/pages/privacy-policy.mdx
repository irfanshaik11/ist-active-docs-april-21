# Privacy Policy

This page explains what information Interstate collects, how it's used, who it's shared with, and how you can exercise your rights over your data.

## What we collect

### Account information
- Email address (if you sign up with email)
- Google account identifier (if you sign in with Google)
- Wallet addresses — the custodial Solana and Monad addresses Turnkey creates for you, plus any external wallets you import
- Referral code and waitlist status

### Trading and activity data
- Every trade you execute: token, amount, price, timestamp, transaction signature
- Current positions and historical portfolio state
- Watchlist, tracked wallets, monitored Twitter handles, monitored Telegram channels
- Settings (slippage, priority fees, MEV mode, themes, language, buy/sell presets)
- Telegram user ID if you use the Interstate Telegram bot

### Technical data
- IP address, device type, and browser (for security and abuse prevention)
- Session logs (for troubleshooting and audit)
- Cookies and local storage that keep you signed in and preserve your preferences

## How we use it

- **To operate the platform** — authenticate you, execute trades, track positions, serve the leaderboard and Arena rewards.
- **To keep the platform safe** — detect abuse, fraud, wash trading, and sanctions risk.
- **To improve the product** — aggregated usage metrics that guide feature work.
- **To comply with law** — meet applicable regulatory and compliance obligations.
- **To communicate with you** — transactional emails and, if you opt in, product updates.

We do **not** sell your personal data.

## Who we share it with

Interstate relies on third-party infrastructure. When you use the relevant feature, data flows to the relevant provider under their own privacy terms.

| Service | What they see |
|---|---|
| **Turnkey** | Wallet public keys and encrypted private-key material |
| **Hyperliquid** | Perpetual futures orders and positions |
| **Polymarket + DFlow** | Prediction market orders and positions |
| **Onramper** | Any fiat-onramp information you enter into their widget |
| **Google** | OAuth sign-in identifiers |
| **Solana and Monad public infrastructure** | Your trades — public by nature of the blockchain |
| **Twitter / X API** | The handles you choose to monitor (to fetch their public tweets) |
| **Telegram** | Your interactions with the Interstate Telegram bot |

We may also share information with law enforcement or regulators when legally required or to protect the platform and its users from harm.

## On-chain data is public

Anything you do on Solana or Monad is recorded on a public blockchain. Addresses, amounts, and transaction history are visible to anyone. Interstate cannot remove or mask on-chain records — the chain itself controls that.

## Cookies and local storage

Interstate uses cookies and browser local storage to keep you signed in, remember preferences (theme, language, quick-buy presets), and detect abuse. You can clear these at any time through your browser, but doing so will log you out and reset preferences.

## Data retention

We retain data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period after, to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain security audit trails. On-chain records are permanent by the nature of blockchains and cannot be deleted.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data Interstate holds about you
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your off-chain data (on-chain records remain)
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Object to** or **restrict** certain processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact Interstate through the channels on the [Support](/support) page.

## Security

Interstate uses industry practices to protect your data: HTTPS end-to-end, HSM-backed wallet custody via Turnkey, encrypted session tokens, and ongoing security review. No system is perfectly secure — use a strong email password, enable two-factor authentication on your email and Google accounts, and protect your device.

## Children

Interstate is not designed for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we learn that a minor has created an account, we will delete it.

## International users

Interstate is accessible globally. If you access the platform from outside your country of residence, your data may be processed in jurisdictions with different privacy laws than your own.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be announced in-product. Continued use after a change means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise any of the rights above, reach Interstate through the channels listed on the [Support](/support) page.
