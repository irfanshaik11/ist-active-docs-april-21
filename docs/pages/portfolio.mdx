# Portfolio

Your portfolio page at [app.interstate.so/portfolio](https://app.interstate.so/portfolio) is the single source of truth for everything you hold and everything you've traded on Interstate.

## What's on the page

| Section | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Active positions** | Every token you currently hold on Solana and Monad, with live current price, market cap, and your unrealized PnL in SOL and % |
| **Realized PnL** | Running total of profit/loss on closed positions, computed at the moment you sold |
| **Total portfolio value** | Sum of all positions in SOL, updated live |
| **PnL chart** | Your portfolio value over time — daily, weekly, or since inception |
| **Trade history** | Every buy and sell, with timestamps, prices, and transaction links to Solscan or the Monad explorer |
| **Top 100 positions** | A ranked view if you hold a long tail — largest positions first |
| **Prediction market positions** | Your Polymarket YES/NO positions, embedded on the same page |

## How PnL is calculated

**Unrealized PnL** uses live WebSocket prices and your on-chain token balance at the current block. It updates automatically as prices move — you don't need to refresh.

**Realized PnL** is recorded at the moment you execute a sell. Interstate stores the realized PnL on the trade record so historical totals stay stable even as prices move.

::: info Multi-wallet trades
If you split a single trade across multiple wallets ([multi-wallet trading](/funding/multi-wallet-trading)), the portfolio filters out the "child" trades so you don't see the same position counted twice. Only the combined parent trade shows up.
:::

## Switching chains

The portfolio is chain-aware. Toggle between **Solana** and **Monad** with the chain switcher in the header — each chain has its own positions, PnL, and history. Monad portfolio features are in **Beta** and cover the MON spot surface; perpetuals and predictions positions are tracked separately.

## Selling from the portfolio

Click any active position to open the sell panel. You can sell:

- By **percentage** (25 / 50 / 100% or custom)
- By **exact token amount**

Sells execute through the same routing as the trade page — Meteora, Raydium, Pump.fun, PumpSwap, or MoonShot, whichever has the active pool. If the pool has migrated since you bought, the sell automatically uses the new pool address.

See [Market orders](/trading/solana-spot/market-orders) for the full sell mechanics.

## Trade history filters

- **By token** — click any token to see every trade you've made on it
- **By wallet** — if you use multiple wallets, filter to one at a time
- **By time range** — standard date pickers

## What's earned automatically

Every trade in your history earned you:

- **Credits** toward your Arena rank — see [How Credits, Ranks & Rewards work](/points)
- **SOL cashback** — a slice of the platform fee paid back in SOL, scaled by rank (10% at DEGEN, up to 35% at TITAN)
- **Referral rewards** if someone signed up with your code and traded — see [Referrals](/arena/referrals)

These don't show as line items in the portfolio itself — check the [Arena overview](/arena/overview) for cashback totals and rank progress.
